Crystals Collector



You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking a specific crystal, you will add a certain number of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your score to the random number. You will lose the game if your score exceeds the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
